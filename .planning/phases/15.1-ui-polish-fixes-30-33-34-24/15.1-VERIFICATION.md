The following items need device or simulator testing to fully confirm goal achievement:

#### 1. Home Screen App Label

**Test:** Build and run DictusApp on iPhone simulator (or device), return to home screen.
**Expected:** App label below the icon reads "Dictus" not "DictusApp".
**Why human:** `CFBundleDisplayName` is verified in the plist, but the rendered home screen label requires a running build.

#### 2. Recording Overlay Button Visual Alignment

**Test:** Open Dictus keyboard in any text field, tap the mic button to begin dictation, observe the overlay appearance.
**Expected:** The check and cancel buttons appear at the exact same vertical position as the mic pill button, with no visual jump on overlay fade-in.
**Why human:** Pixel-level visual alignment requires rendering both ToolbarView and RecordingOverlay simultaneously on device.

#### 3. Swipe-Back Overlay Appearance and Animation Speed

**Test:** Trigger a cold-start dictation from the keyboard (so the app opens to swipe-back overlay). Observe the phone mockup.
**Expected:** The mockup shows a small dark capsule at the top (Dynamic Island representation); the hand swipe animation completes noticeably faster than before (~0.8s cycle).
**Why human:** Visual appearance of the Dynamic Island capsule and subjective animation speed require live rendering.

#### 4. Silent Switch Behavior for Sound Feedback

**Test:** On a physical iPhone, flip the silent switch ON. Trigger a dictation start and stop.
**Expected:** No sounds play when silent switch is on. Flip silent switch OFF and repeat — sounds should play.
**Why human:** `AudioServicesPlaySystemSound` silent-switch behavior requires a physical device with hardware silent switch. Simulators do not have this switch.

#### 5. Sound Playback Timing Relative to Recording

**Test:** Enable sound feedback (Settings > Clavier > Sons > toggle ON). Start and stop a dictation.
**Expected:** Start beep plays audibly before the recording indicator appears (before WhisperKit session); stop chime plays when transcription begins; cancel tone plays on cancel.
**Why human:** Exact timing of sound relative to audio session handoff requires on-device audio verification.
